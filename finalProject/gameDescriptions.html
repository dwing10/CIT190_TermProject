Game Descriptions

Planetary Defense: A space themed arcade shooter.
Eliminate enemy aliens and survive the invasion!

Jumper: A recreation of the Flappy Bird game where avoiding obstacles is the key to success.
Jump over incoming objects to stay alive.
Holding the jump button will allow you to fly.

Zombie Slayer: Your town is under attack from a zombie horde.
Luckily you have found yourself a tank. Shoot the zombies to stay alive
and save your town.
